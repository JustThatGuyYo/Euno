      About

      About StudyHub

      auto_stories

      StudyHub is an all-in-one wellness and productivity tool designed for students. It helps you track your emotional wellbeing, stay organized, and build healthy habits.

      Features

      • check_circleDaily mood check-ins with emoji and slider input
      • check_circlePersonal journal and notes with Markdown support
      • check_circleHabit tracker with weekly analytics
      • check_circlePomodoro timer and study tools
      • check_circleCalendar and to-do list
      • check_circleFlashcard decks
      • check_circleWellness activities and breathing exercises
      • check_circleMood and habit insights with charts
      • check_circleProfessional PDF wellness reports
      • check_circleAuto-save drafts for notes and journals

      Version 3.0 · All data stored locally · No account required

      Privacy Policy

      Privacy Policy

      Your Privacy Matters

      StudyHub is designed with student privacy as a priority.

      Data Storage

      All data you enter in StudyHub — moods, journal entries, habits, tasks, notes — is stored exclusively on your own device using your browser's local storage. No data is sent to any external server, cloud service, or database.

      No Accounts Required

      StudyHub does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information.

      Report Exports

      PDF reports are generated entirely within your browser. Sharing these reports is entirely your choice.

      Data Deletion

      You can clear all stored data at any time via Settings › Clear All Data.
